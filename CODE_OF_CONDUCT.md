# Code of Conduct

Project participants are expected to be respectful, technically honest, and
constructive.

- Critique designs and evidence, not people.
- Give credit for ideas, implementation, testing, review, and debugging.
- Do not claim tests, results, authorship, or permissions that cannot be shown.
- Keep review comments specific and actionable.
- Respect privacy and do not publish private messages or personal information.
- Raise disagreements in the relevant Issue or pull request rather than through
  personal attacks or harassment.

Unacceptable behavior may result in a warning, removal from the discussion, or
loss of collaborator access. Report concerns privately to the repository owner,
`@UILPX`. The maintainer will document project-impacting decisions while keeping
sensitive personal details private.
